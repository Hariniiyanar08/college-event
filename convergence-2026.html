About the Symposium

🎯 Convergence 2026 is a premier academic symposium bringing together students, researchers, and professionals to showcase innovative ideas across multiple disciplines. This prestigious 25th edition celebrates excellence in academics, creativity, and scientific inquiry.

💡 Join us for an inspiring day of learning, networking, and sharing groundbreaking research. Participate in paper presentations, poster exhibitions, project showcases, quizzes, and teaser presentations across Mathematics, Chemistry, Tamil, English, Physics, and other exciting domains.

🚀 This is your platform to shine, inspire, and make your mark in the academic world!

Important Guidelines

✅ Participation Rules

Only first year Engineering students are eligible to participate. One way fare will be provided for outstation participants.

📋 Multiple Events

Each student can participate in a maximum of 2 events - any one individual event and any one team event.

🖥️ Presentation Support

LCD projectors and complete technical support will be provided for all paper and poster presentations.

🏆 Award Categories

Merit certificates for winners in each event and participation certificates provided for all attendees.
